About

From Casablanca to Iowa City

I'm a software engineer originally from Casablanca, Morocco, now based in Iowa City, Iowa. My background is in Computer Science, and I'm currently deepening my skills in artificial intelligence and machine learning — learning how these systems work under the hood, and how to communicate that clearly to people who don't have a technical background.

This site tracks that process. Rather than only listing what I know, I want it to show how I think: how I research a new field, how I break down a complex system, and how I explain it well enough that it's useful to someone else.
